UM Web Accessibility

Web accessibility is an important but overlooked part of web design pertaining to making websites accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities. I worked with other members of The Michigan Data Science Team to analyze and critique the accessibility of the University of Michigan websites. We used Selenium to automate web scraping from web accessibility evaluation sites; then, we analyzed and plotted the data to gain insights from it.
